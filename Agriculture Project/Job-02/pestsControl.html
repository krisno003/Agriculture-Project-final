Our Methodology

4-Step IPM Approach

Integrated Pest Management combines multiple strategies for effective, environmentally friendly pest control

🔍

1. Monitor & Identify

Regular field scouting to identify pests, beneficial insects, and crop health indicators

  • Weekly field inspections
  • Pest identification guides
  • Damage assessment
  • Economic threshold determination
🛡️

2. Prevention First

Implement cultural and biological controls before chemical interventions

  • Crop rotation practices
  • Resistant variety selection
  • Habitat manipulation
  • Proper irrigation management
🎯

3. Targeted Control

Use least toxic methods first, escalating only when necessary

  • Biological control agents
  • Botanical pesticides
  • Microbial insecticides
  • Selective chemical controls
📊

4. Evaluate & Record

Track effectiveness and adjust strategies for continuous improvement

  • Treatment efficacy monitoring
  • Beneficial insect populations
  • Crop yield impact
  • Environmental impact assessment
Insects

Rice Stem Borer

Major pest affecting rice crops, causing deadhearts and whiteheads

Scientific Name: Scirpophaga spp.
Affected Crops: Rice, Wheat
Season: Kharif & Rabi

IPM Control Methods:

  • • Use resistant varieties (IR36, IR64)
  • • Flood irrigation to kill larvae
  • • Release Trichogramma wasps
  • • Apply neem oil sprays

Insects

Cotton Aphids

Sap-sucking insects that cause leaf curling and reduced boll formation

Scientific Name: Aphis gossypii
Affected Crops: Cotton, Okra, Chili
Season: Year-round

IPM Control Methods:

  • • Encourage ladybird beetles
  • • Apply soap water sprays
  • • Use yellow sticky traps
  • • Maintain plant spacing

Diseases

Rice Blast Disease

Fungal disease causing diamond-shaped lesions on leaves and neck blast

Pathogen: Magnaporthe oryzae
Affected Crops: Rice
Conditions: High humidity, 25-30°C

IPM Control Methods:

  • • Use resistant varieties
  • • Avoid excessive nitrogen
  • • Apply silicon fertilizers
  • • Fungicide sprays when needed

Affected Crops: Potato, Tomato
Conditions: Cool, wet weather

Weeds

Parthenium Weed

Invasive weed causing allergies and reducing crop yields significantly

Scientific Name: Parthenium hysterophorus
Affected Crops: All field crops
Impact: Yield loss up to 40%

IPM Control Methods:

  • • Manual weeding before flowering
  • • Use allelopathic crops
  • • Biological control with Zygogramma
  • • Mulching to suppress growth

Nature's Allies

Biological Control Agents

Harnessing beneficial insects and microorganisms for natural pest control

🐞

Ladybird Beetles

Predatory insects that devour aphids, mites, and other soft-bodied pests

Best for: Aphids, spider mites, mealybugs
Release rate: 10-20 per 100 sq.m
Active period: Year-round in tropical climates
🦋

Trichogramma Wasps

Parasitic wasps that lay eggs inside pest insect eggs, preventing hatching

Best for: Stem borers, fruit borers
Release rate: 50,000 per hectare
Active period: During egg-laying season
🕷️

Predatory Mites

Natural enemies of spider mites and other mite species

Best for: Spider mites, russet mites
Release rate: 25,000 per hectare
Active period: Hot, dry conditions
🦠

Bacillus thuringiensis (Bt)

Bacterial insecticide that targets caterpillar pests specifically

Best for: Caterpillars, leaf-eating larvae
Dosage: 1-2 kg per hectare
Safety: Safe for beneficial insects
